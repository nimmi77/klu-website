About KARE

Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education (KARE), established in 1984 by Kalvivallal Thiru T. Kalasalingam, is a pioneer institution located in the lush foothills of the Western Ghats in Tamil Nadu. Originally started as Arulmigu Kalasalingam College of Engineering, it gained the status of a Deemed-to-be University in 2006. With nearly four decades of service in higher education, KARE is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and societal development.

Academic Excellence

KARE offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines such as Engineering, Science, Technology, Management, and Humanities. The curriculum is constantly updated to match global standards, and students are exposed to industry projects, internships, and research opportunities. It is the first institution in India to introduce a dedicated B.Tech program for students with speech and hearing impairment.

Research & Innovation

Kalasalingam University is home to cutting-edge research centers, including:

  • ~National Center for Advanced Research in Discrete Mathematics (DST-funded).

  • ~Interdisciplinary Research Center (IRC) for Material and Life Sciences.

  • ~Centres of Excellence in AI, IoT, and Renewable Energy.

Our faculty and scholars publish in top international journals and collaborate with global research institutions.

Campus & Student Life

KARE boasts a vibrant 163-acre campus with Wi-Fi, digital libraries, smart classrooms, and innovation labs. The campus houses multi-storied hostels, a world-class indoor auditorium, a modern swimming pool, and sporting facilities.
Students thrive in an ecosystem that supports academics, creativity, cultural expression, and community engagement.
